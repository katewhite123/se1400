Who is your website's competition?

My website's competition is all online clothing and design buisnesses. Some I found were I Hate Music, and then on a bigger scale is a online store called He would Love First.

What is something your competing websites do good?

Something my competing websites have are main links to all their apparel. This could look like having links at the tops for Women's, Men's and Kid's clothing.
Since my target audience is mostly one gender and age group I could divide my site into catagories like T-shirts, Seatshirts, Pants, Hats, and Custom.

What is something your competing websites are doing wrong?

Clothing websites sometimes do not showcase their clothing in the best way. Organization and presentation are the most important part of selling a product.

What is something your website will do better?

I want my website to be so organized so that it will showcase my design in the best way.
